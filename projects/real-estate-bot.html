Real Estate Assistant Bot

AI-Powered Real Estate Lead Qualification & Scheduling

Project Overview

The Real Estate Assistant Bot is an intelligent, conversational chatbot built to automate the buying and selling inquiry process for real estate clients. Designed using Voiceflow and integrated with Twilio and Make.com, the system seamlessly collects user information, qualifies leads, and automates email confirmations and calendar scheduling.

Process Flow

The assistant engages users in natural conversation to gather property-related information. For sellers, the bot collects additional property details to help agents receive comprehensive property profiles instantly.
